Powered by AI & real data

How we calculate risk scores

Our machine-learning models analyse millions of historic crime cases and hundreds of environmental indicators. Each neighbourhood receives a score between 1 and 100 (higher = higher relative risk), which we translate into clear colours on the map:

  • Green: lower risk
  • Yellow: medium risk
  • Red: higher relative risk

For every area, the two strongest indicators that contribute to elevated risk are shown transparently in the app, for example: many vehicles per household (more attractive targets) or many single-family houses (lower natural surveillance).
