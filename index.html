How we calculate risk scores

Our machine-learning models analyse millions of historic crime cases and hundreds of environmental indicators. Each neighbourhood receives a score between 1 and 100.

  • Green: lower risk
  • Yellow: medium risk
  • Red: higher relative risk

The two strongest indicators for each area are shown transparently in the app.

  • Trained on official Dutch police data
  • Includes housing density, road networks, income, mobility
  • Scores highlight differences — not predictions
  • Model accuracy above R² = 0.7
