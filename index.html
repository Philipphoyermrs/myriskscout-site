How it works

From map to meaningful insights

MyRiskScout combines real police data with environmental and neighbourhood features to turn your map into a live safety radar.

Step 1

Detect risk around you

Explore colour-coded maps that show relative risk levels per neighbourhood – starting with burglary and bicycle theft in Amsterdam.

Step 2

Understand what drives it

Tap a neighbourhood to see key drivers behind elevated risk, such as many vehicles per household or many single-family homes with lower natural surveillance.

Step 3

Stay informed on the go

Turn on optional background location. If you stay in a higher- risk area for a while, MyRiskScout sends a gentle alert – so you can stay alert and take simple precautions.

Powered by AI & real data

Smart models – transparent insights

Behind every colour on the map is a machine-learning model trained on official police statistics and neighbourhood data. Instead of a black box, MyRiskScout shows you the main drivers behind elevated risk in plain language.

Built for awareness – not fear
  • Risk scores from 0–100 for each neighbourhood.
  • Models tuned to highlight relative differences – not to predict individual incidents.
  • Regular updates as new data becomes available.
Privacy & control

Your data stays your data

MyRiskScout is built to raise awareness while respecting your privacy.

Optional background location

Alerts only work if you enable background location – and you can turn this off at any time in the app settings.

Minimal personal data

We focus on area-level risk, not on tracking individuals. Location is used to trigger alerts, not to build personal profiles.

Clear, honest communication

Risk scores help you compare areas – they never mean that a specific incident will occur. MyRiskScout supports your judgment instead of replacing it.
