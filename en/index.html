Powered by AI & real data

How we calculate risk scores

Our machine-learning models analyse millions of historic crime cases and hundreds of environmental indicators. Each neighbourhood receives a score between 1 and 100 (higher = higher relative risk), which we translate into clear map colours:

  • Green: lower relative risk
  • Yellow: medium risk
  • Red: higher relative risk

Behind every score, the model highlights the two strongest drivers of elevated risk ‚Äì for example:

  • Many vehicles per household ‚Üí more attractive targets
  • Many single-family homes ‚Üí lower natural surveillance
