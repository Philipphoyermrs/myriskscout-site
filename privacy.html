Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

MyRiskScout (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides a mobile application that visualises neighbourhood-level crime risk based on public and environmental data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we use data.

1. What data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Your name
  • Your contact details (unless you contact us yourself)
  • Your movement history or GPS trails
  • Personal profiles for marketing or advertising

2. Location data (optional)

The app uses your device’s location only if you enable it. Location is used temporarily on your device to determine:

  • Which neighbourhood you are currently in
  • Whether a risk alert should be triggered

Your location is not stored, not transmitted, and not used to identify you.

3. Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand app usage (e.g., which screens are visited, how often the app is opened). These analytics:

  • Do not track personal identity
  • Do not use invasive cookies
  • Do not collect precise location

4. What data we use to generate risk scores

Risk scores are based entirely on:

  • Official crime statistics (public or licensed datasets)
  • Neighbourhood-level demographic and socio-economic data
  • Environmental and structural indicators (e.g. housing type, density)

This data is aggregated at neighbourhood level and never tied to individuals.

5. Contact and enquiries

If you contact us (for example via email or contact form), we will use the information you provide (such as your email address and your message) only to respond to your enquiry.

6. Data retention

We store no personal identifiable information from app usage. Anonymous or aggregated analytics are kept only as long as needed to monitor and improve app performance.

7. Third parties

We do not sell or rent any personal data to third parties. If we use third-party services (e.g. analytics providers), we select them with care and, where applicable, ensure appropriate data protection safeguards.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most recent version will always be available on this page, with the date of the latest update indicated at the top.
