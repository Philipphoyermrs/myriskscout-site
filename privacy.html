Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

MyRiskScout (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides a mobile application that visualises neighbourhood-level crime risk based on public and environmental data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we use data.

1. What data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Your identity
  • Your movement history
  • GPS trails or long-term location logs
  • Personal profiles for marketing or advertising

2. Location data (optional)

The app uses your device’s location only if you enable it. Location is used temporarily on your device to determine:

  • Which neighbourhood you are currently in
  • Whether a risk alert should be triggered

Your location is not stored, not transmitted, and not used to identify you.

3. Analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand app usage (e.g., which screens are visited). These analytics:

  • Do not track personal identity
  • Do not use cookies
  • Do not collect precise location

4. What data we use to generate risk scores

Risk scores are based entirely on:

  • Official crime statistics (public sources)
  • Neighbourhood-level demographic data
  • Environmental and structural indicators

This data is never tied to individuals.

5. How long we store data

We store no personal data. Anonymous analytics are kept only as long as needed for app performance optimisation.

6. Third parties

We do not sell or share any personal data with third parties.

7. Children's privacy

MyRiskScout is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your rights

Because we do not store personal identifiable information, most data protection rights (access, deletion) are not applicable. However, you may request clarification anytime.
